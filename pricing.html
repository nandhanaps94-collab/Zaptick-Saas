QUESTIONS

Common questions. Direct answers.

Can't find what you're looking for? Book a 20-minute call and we'll size the right plan with you.

Which plan should I start on?

Most teams start on Starter for the shared inbox, broadcasts and WhatsApp Coexistence, then move to Growth once they need automations, CRM integrations and advanced inbox settings. You only pay Meta's conversation-based message charges on top, based on the destination country.

What's the difference between Growth and Advanced?

Growth gives you 10 shared inbox seats, automated workflows, context-aware chatbot support, and full REST API access. Advanced unlocks custom integrations, unlimited allocations, dedicated SLAs, and dedicated AMs. Talk to sales for customized quotes.

What is the Enterprise plan for?

Enterprise provides custom channel setups, dedicated system infrastructure streams, unlimited agent seats, custom compliance features (such as data localization or logs auditing), and 24/7 technical SLAs.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any point. When you change plans, your billing is prorated automatically by the system.

How does email pricing work — volume vs subscriber mode?

We focus on Monthly Active Contacts (MACs) rather than charging for email volume separately, giving you direct, clear, and simple pricing columns without hidden caps.

Are WhatsApp message charges included in the plan?

No, messaging costs are usage-based according to local Meta rate cards and bill separately. We pass these costs through directly from Meta at cost with 0% markup.
Please note: Across all plans, messaging costs are entirely usage-based according to local Meta rate cards and bill separately from your platform subscription tier.
