Convert Paid Ad Spend Directly Into Conversational Revenue

Bypass form drops and route users directly into pre-filled interactive threads.

Zero-Drop Lead Routing Flow

Traditional marketing funnels lose up to 60% of potential buyers at slow-loading, form-dense landing pages. Click to WhatsApp ads offer a direct shortcut: clicking a sponsored Facebook or Instagram post launches an active, pre-filled WhatsApp thread instantly.

This zero-drop routing ensures that the prospect's mobile profile and verified telephone details are captured the moment they start the thread, creating a friction-free conversion channel.

Algorithmic Inbound Attribution Logs

Attribute customer engagement accurately. ConnectStream connects incoming conversation threads back to Meta Pixel and Conversions API logs, allowing marketers to attribute conversions to specific ad creatives.

With custom parameters tracking, you can monitor exactly which copy variant, target demographic, or ad placement is yielding the lowest acquisition cost.

Instant Product Context Injection

When a customer clicks your product ad, our system automatically tags the incoming thread with product info. Agents see details like the product SKU, size, and category directly on their workspace console, allowing them to provide instant product advice.
